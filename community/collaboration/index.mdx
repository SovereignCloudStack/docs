## Collaborating with issues and pull requests

We use the GitHub flow to track and discuss changes in issues, then propose and
review changes in pull requests. See the
[GitHub documentation](https://docs.github.com/en/free-pro-team@latest/github/collaborating-with-issues-and-pull-requests)
for more details.

## Meetings

### Project updates and general meetings

There is a monthly Community Meeting on the third Friday of the month.

The Lean SCS Operator Coffee is scheduled once per month and welcomes all people that operate or are interested in operating infrastructure.

### Team Meetings

Various Teem Meetings are scheduled throughout the weeks. Some of them are bi-weekly,
some are monthly. Check our public calendar.

### Special interest groups (SIGs) and hacking sessions

Special Interest Groups focus on certain topics.
Please refer to the public calendar above for details.
